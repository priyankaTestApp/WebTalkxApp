Welcome to webtalkx – your ask, record and share solution

Before you get started just a few instructional remarks:

Use the webtalkx platform as a simple, convenient way to request information from someone, retain their video responses and share them with others.

Just use the navigation panel on the left to access the main features of ask, record and share.

You will receive notifications in your inbox as a confirmation of completed steps from you or anyone you invited in the process.

Additionally you will have the ability to message anyone in your webtalkx network by just clicking on the name.

The use cases are endless from offline interviewing of vendors, employees, hiring candidates, customers etc., to entire offline meetings, incl. status checks, requirements gathering, stakeholder surveys, market research and much more.

It’s simple, convenient and personal – start your ask, record and share process now.
